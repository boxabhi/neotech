IGNOU BCA/MCA Projects development in Lucknow

Live Project Training refers to work experience that is relevant to professional development prior to completion of degree. One of the fulfillment for the award of any technical degree like; MCA, B.Tech, BCA, MSc (IT), BSc (IT) is that students must complete at least 4-6 month of Live Project Training. Live Project Training is normally done during the final semesters. Students should know, that Live Project Training plays an important role in the development of the practical and professional skills for employment after completion of their technical degree. Infoseek offers Live Project Training at Lucknow Office for MCA, B.Tech (CS/IT), BCA, IGNOU and other IT students, who are willing to learn new technologies, updates their skills or who are having a dream to work in the Top Companies. This Live Project Training is a complete practical oriented. These programs will cover core and advanced features of each technology. Students from Lucknow, Kanpur and other cities near Lucknow can avail this project training program.
